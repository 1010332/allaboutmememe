Dr. Max Haifei Li (李海飞) was born and raised in China. His hometown is Langtang, a small place where everyone knows everyone. Before he went to Xi'an Jiaotong University in 1986, he had never seen a computer in his life. However, he determined to study computer science and has been in computer field for his professional career. His major areas are software development and machine learning, but he also loves to fix computer hardware. His professional activities can be found at LinkedIn

School activities

He was a good student at schools. His GPA for the undergraduate degree is about 3.1. It is not high GPA, but getting good GPA in a Chinese university in 1980s is very difficult. His GPA for master's degree is about 3.66, but his GPA for his doctoral degree is about 3.92. He always told other people that he is not perfect. Why? Because for his doctoral studies, he got all As except one B+. He always remembers the professor who gave him a B+.
